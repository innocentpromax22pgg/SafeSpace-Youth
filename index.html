About Safe Talk Rwanda

Our Mission

To empower young people (10-30) in Rwanda with accurate, confidential, and stigma-free information about sexual and reproductive health and mental wellbeing.

We believe every young person deserves to make informed choices about their health without fear or judgment.

Our Vision

A Rwanda where all youth feel confident asking health questions and accessing support.

Where stigma is eliminated, information is accurate, and every young person can live a healthy, empowered life.

Inclusion & Accessibility

Safe Talk Rwanda welcomes everyone, including persons with disabilities

🌍 Our Commitment:

Safe Talk Rwanda is committed to ensuring equal access to Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH) and Mental Health information and support for all young people, including persons with disabilities. We believe that every youth deserves access to safe, clear, and understandable information regardless of physical, sensory, or cognitive ability.

Our platform is designed to be inclusive, accessible, and user-friendly for everyone. We actively work to remove barriers that hinder participation and ensure that no young person is left behind.

Our Accessibility Features & Commitments

Visual Accessibility

High contrast text, readable fonts, and resizable content. Compatible with screen readers for users with visual impairments. Easy-to-read formats and clear visual hierarchy.

Hearing Accessibility

Captions and transcripts for all video content. Visual indicators for important alerts. Written alternatives for audio information. Support for hearing aid compatibility.

Motor Accessibility

Full keyboard navigation support. Large touch targets and clickable areas. Simplified controls for users with limited motor function. Voice command compatibility options.

Cognitive Accessibility

Clear, simple language and easy-to-understand instructions. Consistent navigation and predictable layout. Reduced distractions and optional focus modes. Step-by-step guidance for forms and processes.

Language Accessibility

Information available in both English and Kinyarwanda. Plain language explanations of health concepts. Visual aids and diagrams to support understanding.

Mobile & Device Accessibility

Fully responsive design for all devices. Works on older phones with limited capabilities. Low bandwidth options for areas with poor connectivity. Compatible with assistive technologies.

Our Core Commitments

  • Universal Design: We design our platform with accessibility in mind from the beginning, not as an afterthought.
  • Equal Participation: All young people, regardless of ability, should be able to access health information and support services.
  • Dignity & Respect: We treat all users with respect and ensure our language and design choices are inclusive and non-stigmatizing.
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly test our platform with users of different abilities and implement feedback to enhance accessibility.
  • Barrier Removal: We actively identify and remove barriers that prevent people with disabilities from accessing our services.
  • Affordable Access: All services are provided FREE to all youth, ensuring cost is not a barrier to access.
  • Support & Guidance: We provide specialized support for youth with disabilities, including alternative communication methods and personalized assistance.

Did You Know? Many accessibility features benefit everyone. For example, captions help users in noisy environments, and simple language helps when you're tired or stressed. Good accessibility design makes our platform better for all users!
